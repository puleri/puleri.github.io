Quarantine Clue

During quarantine I learned that my family loves playing Clue but playing it on a smart device does not capture the essence of the game. You simply have to see and hear who you are playing with. To solve this problem I created a terminal Clue shuffler that eliminates the problem of the dealer in remote Clue. Playing together on zoom, Q-Clue plays the same as always except every household uses their own board, and players must look away from the screen when dealing.
